Frequently Asked Questions

Are these calculators really free?

Yes, 100% free. No credit card, no email signup, no hidden costs. Use them as many times as you want.

How accurate are the results?

Our calculators use industry-standard formulas and benchmarks from the National Restaurant Association. Results are as accurate as the data you input.

Do I need to create an account?

No account needed. The calculators work instantly in your browser. However, OwnerClone software requires an account to save and track your data over time.
